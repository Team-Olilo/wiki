# Router Configuration Guide

This guide will help you configure your router to work perfectly with your Olilo connection.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- Your Olilo ONT connected and powered on
- A compatible router (see our [recommended routers list](#recommended-routers))
- Your connection credentials from your welcome email
